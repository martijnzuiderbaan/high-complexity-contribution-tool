## Purpose

This canvas supports a structured conversation between a gifted, hoogbegaafde, or high-complexity adult and a manager, employer, HR partner, or coach.

It helps both sides answer:

> Where is complex capacity being converted into sustainable contribution, and where might role or context changes unlock better fit?

## Boundaries

This canvas is not for diagnosing giftedness, measuring IQ, ranking talent, evaluating performance, deciding promotion, assessing burnout, or determining accommodations.

The goal is a role-design conversation and a small work experiment.

## Conversation Principles

1. Talk about person-role-context fit, not about whether someone is "the problem."
2. The adult controls what personal information to disclose.
3. The manager/employer examines role and context conditions, not the person's worth.
4. Separate necessary constraints from avoidable friction.
5. Use concrete examples, not labels alone.
6. End with one bounded 30-day experiment, not a full redesign.
